# Brainrot Index

A dashboard that visualizes how cooked your brain is from consuming TikTok and Instagram content. It maps brainrot dimensions like Meme Entropy, Stimulus Density, Cognitive Depth, and Semantic Coherence to regions of a 3D brain model, showing exactly which parts are getting fried. You can see the specific posts responsible for your current brain state, and check in on your friends to see what content is cooking their brains too. All data is mock -- but the rot is real.
